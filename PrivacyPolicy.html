Privacy Policy for GreenSMS

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Introduction

GreenSMS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access or use the App.

Information We Collect

1. SMS Messages

  • What we collect: We access and read SMS messages on your device to identify receipts, invoices, and related information.
  • Why we collect it: To scan for receipt/invoice keywords and extract URLs from SMS messages containing receipt information.
  • How we use it:
    • We process SMS messages locally on your device to identify receipts and invoices
    • We extract URLs and receipt information from SMS messages
    • We save SMS data to Firebase (only if you are logged in) for organizing your receipts
    • We may forward SMS content to your own email address or Google Drive account (only if you configure this feature)
  • Data storage: SMS data is stored in Firebase Firestore database, associated with your user account. SMS content is also processed locally on your device.
  • Data sharing: SMS data is only shared with your own email/Google Drive account that you configure. We do not share SMS data with third parties.

2. User Account Information

  • What we collect:
    • Name (from Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In)
    • Email address (from Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In)
    • Profile picture (from Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In)
    • User ID (Firebase Authentication ID)
  • Why we collect it: To create and manage your user account, provide personalized services, and track your subscription/trial status.
  • How we use it:
    • To authenticate and identify you in the App
    • To store your preferences and settings
    • To manage your subscription and trial period
    • To associate SMS data with your account
  • Data storage: User account information is stored in Firebase Firestore database.
  • Data sharing: We do not share your account information with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., Firebase for authentication and storage).

3. Device Permissions

  • SMS Permissions: READ_SMS, SEND_SMS, RECEIVE_SMS - Required to scan SMS messages for receipts and invoices.
  • Storage Permissions: READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE - Required to save and read PDF files and receipts on your device.
  • Camera Permission: CAMERA - Required to scan receipts and documents using your device camera.
  • Photo Library Permission: READ_MEDIA_IMAGES - Required to import existing receipts and documents from your photo library.

4. Images and Documents

  • What we collect: Images you select from your photo library or capture using the camera.
  • Why we collect it: To extract text and URLs from images containing receipt information.
  • How we use it: To process images locally on your device to extract receipt information.
  • Data storage: Images are processed locally on your device. We do not store images on our servers unless you explicitly choose to upload them.

5. PDF Documents

  • What we collect: PDF files generated from SMS messages or receipts.
  • Why we collect it: To organize and store your receipts in PDF format.
  • How we use it: PDFs are generated locally on your device and can be saved to your device storage or uploaded to your Google Drive account (if you configure this feature).
  • Data storage: PDFs are stored locally on your device or in your Google Drive account (if you choose to upload them).

6. Device Information

  • What we collect: Device information such as device model, operating system version, and app usage data.
  • Why we collect it: To improve app functionality and troubleshoot issues.
  • How we use it: For analytics and app improvement purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

1. Firebase (Google)

  • Services used: Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Storage
  • Data collected: User authentication data, user profile information, SMS data, app usage data
  • Purpose: To provide authentication, data storage, and cloud services
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

2. Google Sign-In

  • Services used: Google Account authentication
  • Data collected: Name, email address, profile picture
  • Purpose: To allow you to sign in using your Google account
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Apple Sign-In

  • Services used: Apple ID authentication
  • Data collected: Name, email address (if provided)
  • Purpose: To allow you to sign in using your Apple ID
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

4. Google Drive API

  • Services used: Google Drive file storage
  • Data collected: PDF files and documents you choose to upload
  • Purpose: To store your receipts and documents in your Google Drive account
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Gmail API

  • Services used: Gmail email forwarding
  • Data collected: Email content (SMS receipts forwarded to your email)
  • Purpose: To forward SMS receipts to your email address
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
