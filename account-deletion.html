Account Deletion Request

App Name: GreenSMS

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from GreenSMS, please follow these steps:

Request Deletion via Email

To delete your account, please send us an email with your deletion request:

  1. Send an email to: psayantan08@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - GreenSMS"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address or user ID
    • Your name (as registered in the app)
    • Confirmation that you want to delete your account
  4. We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email

⚠️ Important: Before Deleting Your Account

  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All your data will be permanently deleted
  • You will lose access to all receipts, invoices, and SMS data stored in your account
  • Any active subscriptions will be cancelled
  • Make sure to export or download any important data before deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User Account Information:
    • Name
    • Email address
    • Profile picture
    • User ID
    • Account preferences and settings
  • SMS Data:
    • All SMS messages stored in Firebase
    • SMS content, sender information, and timestamps
    • Extracted URLs and receipt information
    • Processed receipt data
  • App Data:
    • Generated PDF documents stored in Firebase
    • Receipt organization data
    • App usage history
    • Subscription and trial information

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and operational purposes, we may retain certain information for a limited period:

  • Legal Compliance: We may retain data required by law or for legal proceedings (typically up to 7 years)
  • Fraud Prevention: Transaction records may be retained to prevent fraud (typically up to 2 years)
  • Backup Systems: Data in backup systems will be deleted during regular backup rotation (typically within 90 days)

Note: Retained data will be anonymized and not associated with your account.

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated and you will lose access
  • Within 30 days: All account data will be permanently deleted from active systems
  • Within 90 days: Data will be removed from backup systems
  • Legal retention: Any legally required data will be retained as specified above

Local Device Data

Important: Deleting your account does NOT automatically delete data stored locally on your device:

  • PDF files saved on your device storage
  • Images stored locally
  • App cache and local preferences

To remove local data, you can:

  • Uninstall the app (this will remove all local data)
  • Clear app data through your device settings
  • Manually delete PDF files and images from your device storage

Third-Party Services

If you used third-party services through our app, you may need to delete data separately:

  • Google Drive: Files uploaded to your Google Drive account will remain unless you delete them manually
  • Gmail: Emails forwarded to your Gmail account will remain in your email inbox
  • Firebase: All data stored in Firebase will be deleted as part of account deletion
